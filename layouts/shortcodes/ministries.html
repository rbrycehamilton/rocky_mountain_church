During nursery and children’s church, we have singing, an engaging, age-appropriate lesson, and a fun activity or craft.

Ladies' Ministry


Women meet bi-weekly at 4pm for our Digging Deeper Bible study at 530 S 500 W in Brigham City. Join us for a time of fellowship, prayer, and digging into God’s Word! We also have periodic women’s activities.

Childcare is provided.

Men's Ministry


Men meet bi-weekly at 6pm for our Digging Deeper Bible study at 530 S 500 W in Brigham City. Join us for a time of fellowship, prayer, and digging into God’s Word! We also have periodic men’s activities.

Ministries We Support

Pioneer Bible Camp


"Pioneer Bible Camp exists to introduce people of all ages to Jesus Christ as Savior and to disciple and encourage believers in their Christian Walk."

Key Radio


"Key Radio's purpose is simple - to proclaim the Good News of Jesus Christ throughout Utah and beyond. This was the mission of Key Radio since it changed to a Christian talk format back in 1988, and it is the same today. Yes, that Good News is that good"

Pregnancy Care Center


The Pregnancy Care Center in Brigham City seeks to provide help for stressful pregnancies and resources for parents and soon-to-be parents.
